About the Book

MM Sports Romance · Jack King

Jack Murphy is a twenty-nine-year-old flanker clinging to the last years of a career that never quite became what he promised himself it would. Marco Santos is the brilliant young fullback who makes him want to be better. Coach Michael Ashford is the man who built them both — and who wants things he was never supposed to want.

What begins as tension on the training pitch becomes something none of them can contain. In a sport built on brotherhood and silence, three men discover that the most dangerous play is the one where you stop pretending.
